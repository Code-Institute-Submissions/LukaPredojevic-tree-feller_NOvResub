How to play

Welcome to Tree Feller a game in which you collect wood. The game is very simple and only has a few core mechanics: You gain wood by pressing on the tree. Wood you can spent on upgrades in the store. Each upgrade gives you a different boost. Some upgrades even give you passive wood per second.
